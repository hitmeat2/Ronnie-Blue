About

Award-winning senior designer living in London, United Kingdom.

I consider myself a designer of many trades with more than 10 years of experience in the design world. I am a UI / UX, Illustrator, and Graphic Designer and have my Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from the University of London in UK. I have worked with all kinds of customers and companies, learning from each one of them while enhancing my professional skills.

 

I’m very passionate about illustration, whether traditional or digital, and I strongly believe that it is an essential part of communication to effectively convey messages. That’s why working with any type of interface using graphic design is an enjoyable challenge with lots of room for me to continue growing in my profession.
